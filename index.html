How to access Fort Huachuca

  • Please Obtain Pre-Clearance! Save yourself the hassle of a delay at the gate by obtaining your pre-clearance authorization online. Visit This Link To Obtain Pre-Clearance. Choose "Army - Fort Huachuca - Visitor Pass". Fill out the appropriate information and then select "Unclassified - MWR Event".
  • Bring a government-issued photo ID (REAL ID–compliant ID is required for vetting per Fort Huachuca guidance).
  • If you do not have a DoD credential (CAC/Military ID), you should expect a background check prior to unescorted access.
  • Follow the official Visitor Access page for current rules, locations, and updates.
  • Vehicle operators should have a valid driver’s license and proof of insurance; vehicles may be subject to inspection.
  • If you’re a non-U.S. citizen, the official policy indicates you must be escorted on the installation.
Visitor Control Center (VCC) / Gate hours:
Buffalo Soldier Gate: open 24/7
Van Deman Gate & Visitor Control Center: Mon–Fri 5:30 AM–5:30 PM (closed weekends/holidays)
We’re intentionally keeping this page “high level.” For complete eligibility details and the most current instructions, use the official Visitor Access page link above.

What to bring

  • Government photo ID (REAL ID–compliant ID recommended/required for vetting per official guidance).
  • Extra time for installation access if you don’t already have a pass.
  • If driving: driver’s license + proof of insurance.
  • Optional: your favorite game(s), miniatures, dice, and measuring tools — but you can also just show up and play.
